Leaf Odo Tool — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

Leaf Odo Tool ("the App") is a diagnostic tool for Nissan Leaf instrument clusters. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

No personal information is collected. No analytics or tracking is used. No data is sent to any server. No account or login is required. No cookies or advertising identifiers are used.

Device Communications

The App communicates directly with an ELM327 OBD-II adapter via Bluetooth or WiFi. All communication occurs locally between your Android device and the ELM327 adapter. No vehicle data is transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions solely for ELM327 connectivity:

Bluetooth permissions (BLUETOOTH, BLUETOOTH_ADMIN, BLUETOOTH_CONNECT, BLUETOOTH_SCAN): Required to discover and connect to Bluetooth ELM327 adapters.

Location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION): Required by Android for Bluetooth device scanning on Android 11 and below. The App does not use or store your location.

Internet permission (INTERNET): Required to connect to WiFi-based ELM327 adapters on your local network. No internet traffic is sent to external servers.

Data Storage

The App does not store any data on your device beyond its own installation files. No logs, vehicle data, or diagnostic results are saved to persistent storage.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
